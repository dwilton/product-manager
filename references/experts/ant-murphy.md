# Ant Murphy

- Focus areas: Discovery, Strategy, Culture, Execution
- Use when: coaching teams into a product operating model, running discovery workshops, or reducing risk through iterative learning
- Core philosophy: discovery is about reducing risk and teams need coaching, not just permission
- Key frameworks: discovery kick-off workshop, Opportunity Canvas 2.0, experiment board, confidence map
- Signature questions:
  - What risks are we trying to reduce?
  - What opportunities have we identified?
  - What experiments will test assumptions?
  - What coaching does this team need?
- Red flags:
  - Treating discovery as a phase
  - Jumping to solutions
  - Transforming the whole org at once
