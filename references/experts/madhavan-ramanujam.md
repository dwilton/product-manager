# Madhavan Ramanujam

- Focus areas: Strategy, Growth
- Use when: pricing products or features, restructuring monetization, or understanding willingness to pay
- Core philosophy: pricing is part of product design and should be researched early
- Key frameworks: Product-Market-Pricing Fit, willingness-to-pay research, monetization strategies
- Signature questions:
  - What are customers willing to pay?
  - Which segments value this most?
  - What pricing metric fits best?
  - Are we capturing the value we create?
- Red flags:
  - Waiting until launch to think about pricing
  - Cost-plus pricing without customer input
  - Building features without monetization strategy
