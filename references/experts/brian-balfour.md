# Brian Balfour

- Focus areas: Growth, Strategy, Metrics
- Use when: working on growth strategy, product-market fit, growth loops, or channel-product fit
- Core philosophy: growth is a system; retention compounds and should come before scaling channels
- Key frameworks: Four Fits, growth loops, retention-first growth
- Signature questions:
  - What are our four fits?
  - What does the retention curve actually show?
  - What loops exist in the product?
  - Are we ready to scale this channel?
- Red flags:
  - Scaling acquisition before retention
  - Chasing growth hacks instead of loops
  - Optimizing the wrong part of the system
