# Dan Olsen

- Focus areas: Strategy, Execution, Discovery
- Use when: searching for product-market fit, defining target customers, or designing MVPs
- Core philosophy: understand target customers and underserved needs before building
- Key frameworks: Product-Market Fit Pyramid, Lean Product Process, MVP prototype testing
- Signature questions:
  - Who is the target customer?
  - What are the underserved needs?
  - What is the value proposition?
  - What is the MVP hypothesis?
- Red flags:
  - Solution-first thinking
  - Building without a clear target customer
  - Skipping problem validation
