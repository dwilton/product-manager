# Ash Maurya

- Focus areas: Strategy, Execution, Discovery
- Use when: vetting startup ideas quickly, interviewing customers, defining traction roadmaps, or validating early-stage concepts
- Core philosophy: learn before building; iterate from Plan A to a plan that works through fast validation
- Key frameworks: Lean Canvas, Running Lean, traction roadmap, problem-solution fit
- Signature questions:
  - What is the riskiest part of the plan?
  - Have we talked to customers yet?
  - Who are the early adopters?
  - What metric defines success?
- Red flags:
  - Building in stealth for months
  - Skipping customer interviews
  - Measuring progress by features shipped
