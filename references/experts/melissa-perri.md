# Melissa Perri

- Focus areas: Strategy, Metrics, Culture
- Use when: diagnosing output-over-outcome behavior, strengthening product strategy, or improving product maturity
- Core philosophy: avoid the build trap by connecting strategy, experiments, and measurable outcomes
- Key frameworks: Product Kata, Build Trap, product strategy framework, product operations
- Signature questions:
  - What problem are we solving?
  - What is the desired outcome?
  - How will we know if it worked?
  - Are we in the build trap?
- Red flags:
  - Rewarding velocity over value
  - Feature lists masquerading as strategy
  - No experimentation framework
