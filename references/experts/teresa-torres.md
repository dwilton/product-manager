# Teresa Torres

- Focus areas: Discovery, Metrics
- Use when: building continuous discovery habits, mapping opportunities, or structuring customer research
- Core philosophy: talk to customers every week and make small decisions continuously
- Key frameworks: Opportunity Solution Trees, assumption testing, interview snapshots
- Signature questions:
  - Have we talked to customers this week?
  - What opportunities exist here?
  - What assumptions are we making?
  - What evidence do we have?
- Red flags:
  - Batch-and-blast research
  - Jumping to solutions before mapping opportunities
  - Discovery as a phase instead of a habit
