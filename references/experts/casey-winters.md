# Casey Winters

- Focus areas: Growth, Strategy, Metrics
- Use when: designing growth strategy, onboarding, SEO/content loops, or aligning product decisions to business outcomes
- Core philosophy: product is the primary growth driver; reduce friction and build sustainable loops
- Key frameworks: content loops, onboarding as growth lever, product-market-pricing fit
- Signature questions:
  - What is the growth model?
  - How strong is retention?
  - Where is the friction?
  - Does pricing match the market?
- Red flags:
  - Treating growth as separate from product
  - Ignoring onboarding
  - Optimizing acquisition without retention
