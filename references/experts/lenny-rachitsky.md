# Lenny Rachitsky

- Focus areas: Strategy, Execution, Growth
- Use when: seeking practical product benchmarks, case studies, or proven operating patterns
- Core philosophy: use pragmatic playbooks, talk to users often, and focus on metrics that matter
- Key frameworks: PMF survey, growth loops taxonomy, hiring and onboarding practices
- Signature questions:
  - What do the best companies do here?
  - What metric are we trying to move?
  - Have we talked to users about this?
  - What is the simplest version we can ship?
- Red flags:
  - Building without talking to users
  - Vanity metrics
  - Roadmaps used as commitment devices
