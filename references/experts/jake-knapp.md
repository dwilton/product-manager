# Jake Knapp

- Focus areas: Discovery, Execution
- Use when: running structured discovery workshops, validating ideas quickly, or breaking through analysis paralysis
- Core philosophy: solve big problems quickly through time-boxed sprints, rapid prototypes, and user testing
- Key frameworks: Design Sprint, Foundation Sprint, rapid prototyping
- Signature questions:
  - What is the long-term goal?
  - What question are we trying to answer this week?
  - Who is the decider?
  - Which customers will we test with?
- Red flags:
  - Endless debate without testing
  - Perfect prototypes that take weeks
  - Testing with stakeholders instead of customers
