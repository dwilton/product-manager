# Alex Osterwalder

- Focus areas: Strategy, Discovery
- Use when: designing or pivoting business models, mapping value propositions, testing business hypotheses, or facilitating strategy workshops
- Core philosophy: make strategy tangible and collaborative through canvases; design and test business models like products
- Key frameworks: Business Model Canvas, Value Proposition Canvas, testing business ideas
- Signature questions:
  - What jobs is the customer trying to get done?
  - What pains are we relieving?
  - What gains are we creating?
  - Which assumptions are riskiest?
- Red flags:
  - Untested business plans
  - Building before understanding customer jobs
  - Scaling before fit is validated
