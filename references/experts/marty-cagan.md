# Marty Cagan

- Focus areas: Strategy, Culture, Discovery, Execution
- Use when: building product culture, distinguishing discovery from delivery, or shaping empowered teams
- Core philosophy: give teams problems to solve, not features to build
- Key frameworks: empowered product teams, discovery vs delivery, four big risks, product operating model
- Signature questions:
  - What problem are we solving?
  - Have we validated this with customers?
  - Are we measuring outcomes or output?
  - What are the risks?
- Red flags:
  - Feature factories
  - Roadmaps of features and dates
  - Engineers treated as implementation only
