# Personas



A **Persona** represents a key user type who interacts with your product. It captures goals, behaviors, pain points, and context to help teams build empathy and make customer-centered decisions.

Use this template to document a single persona based on real customer research — not assumptions or stereotypes. Keep it evidence-based, focused on goals and behaviors, and directly applicable to product decisions.
