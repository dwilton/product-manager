# Market Requirements Document (MRD)



A **Market Requirements Document (MRD)** defines what the market needs before defining how we'll build it. It focuses on market opportunities, customer segments, competitive landscape, and business case to provide the strategic foundation for product decisions.

Use this template to validate market opportunity, align stakeholders on target segments, and establish business rationale before investing in detailed product requirements.

Keep it decision-ready — clear enough for leadership to approve market direction and resource allocation. Link to supporting research in the Appendix.
