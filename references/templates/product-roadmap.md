# Product Roadmap



A **Product Roadmap** communicates which customer problems we're addressing and the outcomes we expect. It aligns everyone on customer value without locking us into feature commitments.

Use this template to organize initiatives by time horizon (Now, Next, Later) based on confidence and validation. Focus on outcomes over outputs — solve problems, not build features.

Update monthly on progress and quarterly on direction.

## Roadmap Principles

**Outcomes over outputs** – We solve problems, not build features

**Evidence-based** – Every item backed by customer evidence

**Adaptive** – Learning changes direction, and that's expected

**Confidence-based** – Clarity decreases with time horizon

### What NOT to Include

- ❌ Feature names or descriptions
- ❌ Delivery dates for solutions
- ❌ Technology commitments
- ❌ "Requirements" or specifications

### Update Cadence

- **Monthly** – What we learned, how outcomes are trending
- **Quarterly** – Outcome achievement review, direction updates
- **As needed** – Major pivots or new evidence
