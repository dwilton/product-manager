# Product Requirements Document (PRD)



A **Product Requirements Document (PRD)** captures the context, problem, solution, and expected outcomes of an initiative. It starts as a concise one-pager, decision-ready and focused on why this matters, how it helps customers, and how success will be measured.

Use this template to align the team on what to build and why. Keep it focused — link to research, designs, or deeper documentation in the Appendix.

As complexity or risk increases, the one-pager evolves into a full PRD. Treat it as a living document, updated as new insights emerge during discovery and delivery.
