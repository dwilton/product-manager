# Problem Definition or Opportunity Brief



A **Problem Definition** (or **Opportunity Brief**) clearly articulates a customer problem or business opportunity before jumping to solutions. It ensures the team understands what problem we're solving and why it matters before deciding how to solve it.

Use this template during discovery to align stakeholders, validate assumptions, and build a shared understanding of the problem space. Keep it evidence-based, focused, and written from the customer's perspective.
