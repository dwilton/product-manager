# Product Strategy



A **Product Strategy** defines how we win: the choices we make about where to play, how to compete, and what to prioritize. It connects company goals to customer outcomes and guides decisions at every level.

Use this template to create a simple and repeatable strategy that your team can explain and apply. Treat it as a living document, revisiting at least twice a year as context evolves.
