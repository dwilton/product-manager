# Product Metrics Dashboards



A **Product Metrics Dashboard** provides a single view of the metrics that matter most to product success. It tracks leading and lagging indicators, customer behavior, business performance, and product health to provide visibility into performance, track progress toward strategic outcomes, and surface early warnings and opportunities.

Use this template as a guide for structuring your dashboard — whether in a BI tool, analytics platform, or embedded views. Keep it focused, actionable, and tied directly to outcomes.

Link to data sources and detailed analysis in the Resources section.

## **Dashboard Principles**

- **Outcome-focused** — Metrics tie to business and customer outcomes
- **Actionable** — Every metric suggests a decision or action
- **Accessible** — Available to the whole product team
- **Timely** — Updated frequently enough to inform decisions
- **Contextual** — Show trends, comparisons, and targets

## **How to Use This Dashboard**

- **Daily:** Check product health (uptime, errors, load times) and active users
- **Weekly:** Review engagement, activation, and feature adoption; investigate anomalies
- **Monthly:** Deep dive into retention, revenue, funnels, and cohort performance
- **Quarterly:** Assess OKR progress and adjust strategy
