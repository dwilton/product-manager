# Assumptions Map

- Use when: assumptions must be plotted by evidence and importance to reveal what to validate first
- Phase: Product
- Type: Prompt
- Rating: 5/5

## Output Rules

- Extract explicit and implicit assumptions.
- Map them on a 2x2 matrix:
  - evidence: low to high
  - importance: low to high
- Highlight high-importance, low-evidence assumptions as validation priorities.
