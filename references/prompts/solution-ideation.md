# Solution Ideation

- Use when: solution-focused HMWs need divergent idea generation before evaluation
- Phase: Product
- Type: Prompt
- Rating: 5/5

## Output Modes

- Sketches: 2-3 concept descriptions
- Brainwriting: 4-6 rapid ideas
- Lightning Demos: 2-3 inspirations from analogous products or services

## Output Rules

- Group all outputs under the relevant HMW.
- Keep this stage divergent.
- Do not cluster, prioritize, or evaluate ideas.
