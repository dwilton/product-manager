# Design Critique

- Use when: a team needs structured critique of design work tied to user needs and product goals
- Phase: Product
- Type: Prompt
- Rating: 5/5

## Output Structure

- Artifact reviewed
- Objective or problem
- What works
- What to improve
- Questions or suggestions
- Next steps or decisions

## Output Rules

- Focus on usability, accessibility, consistency, and goal alignment.
- Avoid personal taste as justification.
